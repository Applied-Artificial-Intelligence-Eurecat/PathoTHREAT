Zoom in and out using the scroll wheel, or by pinching on touch screens. To see the entire graph, click on the Autoscale button. Hovering over a node or an edge will highlight it and display its information. Tapping or clicking a node will select itself and all its successors, that is, all the nodes and edges that can be reached from this node. When a node is selected, only its successors can be hovered to see their information. To deselect a node, tap or click on a blank point of the graph. Pressing the Export button will download the graph's data into a file.
